     PC PROBLEM/ERROR
	>PC won't boot to BIOS

	*POSSIBLE CAUSES*
- faulty RAM (or something wrong related to RAM)
	>try transfering to another slot or replace it
- faulting PCI cards
- misplaced front panel connectors
-missing power supply cables
	>check the 24-pin ATX cable and the 4-pin or 8-pin CPU connector
- the CPU is dead
- PSU not working properly
